Modern Style

Modern Style is a style that has been popular since the industrial revolution in Europe. because in that era There have been economic problems and many new technologies have been thought of. Therefore, from the original decoration of houses with a lot of fine details, emphasizing beauty and extravagance, these details were cut down. that down Emphasis on a model that is more practical (Functional) The colors used in that modern style Not often used Often play with only 2-3 colors in the room. Most of the time, white is used as the main color scheme. The white tone will help make the room feel airy. However, if you don't like white because you feel that it's difficult to care for. You can try gray or black, and then add some bright colors.
