Minimal Style

If it comes to decorating people's homes nowadays Another style that is getting so much attention that I can't help but think about it. Decorating the house in a minimalist style or Minimal Style that takes the main idea from simplicity, a little bit of utility. But it gives the atmosphere that is airy, open, relaxed and good looking. Incredibly unique.

Minimal style doesn't have a specific color scheme, but mostly focuses on basic colors like white, black, gray, etc. because it feels light. not too flashy But you can use other tones as well. But keep the tone in three shades: dark, neutral, and light. For example, choose black as a darker shade. white to light So the middle color is gray and so on.

Modern Style

Modern Style is a style that has been popular since the industrial revolution in Europe. because in that era There have been economic problems and many new technologies have been thought of. Therefore, from the original decoration of houses with a lot of fine details, emphasizing beauty and extravagance, these details were cut down. that down Emphasis on a model that is more practical (Functional)

The colors used in that modern style Not often used Often play with only 2-3 colors in the room. Most of the time, white is used as the main color scheme. The white tone will help make the room feel airy. However, if you don't like white because you feel that it's difficult to care for. You can try gray or black, and then add some bright colors.
