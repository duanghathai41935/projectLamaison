Techniques for organizing a desk according to Feng Shui principles

1. According to Feng Shui principles. Do not arrange the desk so that it faces the door or opposite the door. because it is believed to bring fortune and money Including the prestige flows outside the work room.

2. We should always maintain the cleanliness of the desk. Don't let it get cluttered or dirty.

3. Avoid tight corners. because according to Feng Shui principles We should not turn the desk towards the wall. In addition to creating discomfort when sitting and working. It is also believed to be an unfortunate corner. Make the brain not clear.

4. Placement of things on the desk is important as well. which according to feng shui Suggest that we place books or items that we use regularly. on the left Part of decorating a room or a picture frame Move to the right side instead. To be a good encouragement when you have to sit and work stressful

5. Do not arrange your desk under the beam. because there is something above that head According to feng shui, it is believed that there is a lot of energy over it. It makes me unable to think of work, my brain is not exhausted, whatever I do, I get stuck. Not happy at work
